About Sophie

About

Sophie is a clinical physiotherapist specialising in musculoskeletal assessment, manual therapy and rehabilitation. She provides practical, evidence-based care to help clients recover from injury and improve long-term movement and function.

Sophie works one-to-one with clients to develop realistic treatment plans, focusing on self-management, tailored exercises and gradual return to activity.

Approach

Sophie's approach combines hands-on treatment with education, movement retraining and progressive exercise programmes designed around each client's goals.

Qualifications

  • BSc (Hons) Physiotherapy
  • MSc Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation
  • HCPC registered physiotherapist
  • Member of the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP)
  • Ongoing CPD in manual therapy and exercise rehabilitation

Team

Sophie works with trusted local partners and can provide referrals to specialist services when needed. She offers in-clinic and remote consultations.
